About

Hello and thanks for visiting! I started my progarmming career in high school where I studied areas of computer science from binary code to C++, programming simple games and building web pages for friends. Since then I've gradually taught myself more and more about computer science, programming, and software development. After high school, I became fascinated with the mysteries of the mind and received a degree in Psychology from the University of North Carolina at Wilmington.

After college I started working within a small corporation that bought and sold industrial manufacturing equipment. I was hired on as a procurement agent and quickly worked my way into sales as I developed relationships, growing old accounts and establishing new ones. Sales was exciting and lucrative, but it wasn't long before I found my way to the source code of our company website and began making small adjustments to improve the user experience. Before long I took charge of our inventory and advertising databases while maintaining a healthy sales record.

It wasn't long before I rediscovered my passion for coding and decided to enroll in the Full Stack Software Development Program at UNCC. I enjoy every second I spend coding and am on the look-out for new and exciting opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute with a passionate and driven team.
